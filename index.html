    My Steps On Creating Functional Solutions

    Step 1. Idenify and understand the problem: Know what gives your potential customer sleepless nights and why it does. It must solve a critical problem. My previous years of experience researching and analysing businesses will help to deliver high valued products to your users.
    Step 2. Think of the solution: A functional and user-friendly solution will always stand the test of time. Start by asking, what are my potential users already using? Understanding your potential users pain points will help you focus on creating a product that gives them peace of mind.
    Step 3. Design your solution: Understand the patterns in solutions that currently exist, find reviews, solve the customer complaints on your design canvas.
